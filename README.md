# Awesome Living in Korea for Foreigners [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> The practical companion every foreign resident in Korea needs. Plain-language help with the systems that don't come with instructions (visas, housing, insurance, taxes), honest picks on the services and places that actually work, and the primary sources behind every claim.

A curated list of resources for the 2.84 million foreign residents living in Korea. Government portals, trustworthy guides, calculators, and directories. Maintained by [Seoulstart](https://seoulstart.com), who run a multilingual practical-guide site for foreign residents in Korea.

**Who this list serves:** foreigners moving to Korea, expats living in Korea, foreign residents, international students on D-2 visas, foreign workers on E-7 and E-9 visas, English teachers on E-2 visas, multicultural families, F-4 Korean-heritage diaspora, F-6 marriage visa holders, and digital nomads exploring Korea. If you're searching "moving to Korea", "living in Korea as a foreigner", "expat life in Korea", or "Korea for foreigners", you're in the right place.

Also available in: [한국어](README.ko.md) · [Tiếng Việt](README.vi.md) · [Filipino](README.fil.md) · [Русский](README.ru.md) · [中文](README.zh.md)
